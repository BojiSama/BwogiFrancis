SYS Medical Emergency App

A mobile app that helps increase access to healthcare in Uganda by offering a platform where one can call for assistance from emergency contacts, access first-aid instructions, and get to contact the nearest healthcare centers for further assistance.

Features

  • Panic button to call for assistance from emergency contacts
  • Database of first-aid instructions
  • Voice assistant to help users in an emergency
  • Information about the nearest healthcare centers based off live-location

How to use

  1. Download the app and register for an account.
  2. Enter your emergency contacts.
  3. Press the panic button in an emergency.
  4. The voice assistant will help you through the process.
